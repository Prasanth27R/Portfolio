Mikazuki Augus, also known as " Mika ", is the main protagonist of Mobile Suit Gundam: Iron-Blooded Orphans, the fourteenth installment of the Gundam franchise.
Mika is a member of Tekkadan and the unit leader of the Commando unit,
as well as the right-hand man and best friend of Tekkadan's leader Orga Itsuka.
